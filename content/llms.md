# newschematic.org

> Personal site of Christopher Boette — senior software engineer (TypeScript, React, Node.js, Python, AWS) based in Pittsburgh, PA. Currently at ServiceNow (via data.world acquisition, 2025). Writing spans orbital mechanics, Rust, LLM tooling, agentic AI, and the intersection of software and physical making. CMU background (Masters in Entertainment Technology, B.A. Creative Writing).

The site is a technical blog and project portfolio. Christopher has ~12 years of professional software experience, with depth in frontend product engineering, full-stack TypeScript/React, and LLM integrations.

Current making interest: a web service that generates STEP files from user-specified parameters, learning CAD to validate the concept.
