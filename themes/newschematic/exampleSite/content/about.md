I'm Christopher. I've been writing software for about eleven years, mostly at the web layer and increasingly at the intersection of large models and physical tools. I keep this site as a place for longer writing than a tweet and shorter thinking than a book.

The name is literal. A *new schematic* is the answer to a problem that didn't have one before — a drawing that didn't exist yesterday, and explains something that was unclear. Most of what I write here is an attempt at one.

I live in Pittsburgh with my wife, who runs a bakery, and three small children who are mostly not interested in software. You can reach me at chris@example.com.
